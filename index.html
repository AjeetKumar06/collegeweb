About Our HIM

HINDU INSTITUTE OF MANAGEMENT ( HIM )

The Hindu Institute of Management is one of the oldest Management Institute in Delhi-NCR. Established in the year 1994 ,by The Sonepat Hindu Educational and Charitable Society, Sonepat(Delhi-NCR).

The Institute conducts regular Post Graduate & Under Graduate programs like MBA, MCA, BBA & BCA under affiliation of D.C.R.U.S.T Murthal (Govt. University, Accredited ‘A’ Grade by NAAC) and duly approved by AICTE,New Delhi. Institute located is just opposite the Sonepat Railway Station and also close to Bus Stand.

The Society has been active since 1914 and has established 14 Institutions offering programmes at Secondary, Graduate and Post-Graduate levels.
