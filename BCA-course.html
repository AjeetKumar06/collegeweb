Bachelor of Computer Application ( BCA )

Programme Structure : ( BCA )
It is a semester based 3-year undergraduate program which comprises of six semesters. The course bridges the gap between institute and industry by emphasizing on latest computer applications. HIM runs this course under affiliation with DCRUST, Murthal. The course provides a foundation in mathematics, computer science and management skills applied to computer science. In this course the students will be exposed to various areas of computer applications including the latest developments keeping abreast with the industry The Institute has well-equipped lab for the department with the latest software and computers.


BCA is a 3-year full time degree programme which includes 6 semesters with training (industrial) after 5th semester. The UG degree is awarded by DCRUST, Murthal
Eligibility Criteria : Candidate of any stream (Science, Commerce or Arts) securing 50% or more (45 % marks only for SC/ST/OBC candidate) at 10+2 class. Student must be required intermediate Knowledge of Computer operator.

  1. Reservation of seats is strictly according to the Haryana State Government guidelines.
  2. Management Quota/NRI 25% seats have been reserved as the management quota.
  3. Admission to these seats will be made at the institute level based on merit.

Course Information

BCA is Full-time Technical UG course. The duration is 3 years ( 6 semester ).

BCA subjects include Database Management systems, software engineering, Computer Networking, Operating systems, Web technology and computer languages such as HTML & Css, C, C++, C#, Java.

FAQ

What type of jobs will be offered after the BCA ?

After the BCA student can select the field of Software development, website designing & developing, digital marketing, software testing, database administrator, BPO, Banking jobs, Government jobs, etc.

What is the admission procedure to take admission in BCA ?

Candidate eligible for admission will fill the online application form available on the college website and pay a registration fee of Rs. 1,000/- to take provisional admission. We can not be force to pay complete fees at the time of admission.

Candidate can offers to pay annual fees in two installment.
